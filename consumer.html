Buyer Protection

Buyer protection is our service to consumers, where-in their funds are held securely with us. Funds are released to Merchant, only after Buyer confirms receipt of service delivery. Buyer is assured of service delivery or refund. Buyer protection applies only to certain class of Merchants

Refunds
