Flexible Payout Choices to Suit Your Business Needs

Settlement Options

Payseq offers flexible settlement options to suit your business needs. Choose full payments upfront, per-transaction settlements, or split payments with vendors for smooth and hassle-free reconciliation.

01

Pre-Paid TDR

Receive the full amount your customer pays. Pre-pay the TDR at your convenience with no deductions. Enjoy hassle-free accounting and reconciliation.

02

Per-Transaction Settlement

Don’t want aggregated settlements? Choose to receive settlements for each transaction individually, giving you more control and transparency.

03

Split Settlements

Let your customers make a single payment while the amount is automatically split between you and your vendor. Both parties receive their share at the same time.
